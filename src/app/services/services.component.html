The installers find Acetech a constant point of reference during all the installation phases, with the utmost respect for all the roles of the supply chain. Upon request, we also take care of installation, positioning and first start-up, usually the most delicate step.

Modular products with competitive maintenance costs

Our anti-robbery compasses and our metal detectors can be completely dismantled and easily reassembled even in environments that are difficult to access , such as historic buildings or high floors. Essential feature that combines with ease of use and configuration.

Our job, in fact, is to develop intuitive and simple assembly products. This is a determining element in the evaluation of an investment in anti-robbery compasses or metal detectors, because in addition to having very competitive prices , Acetech products have extremely low post-sale maintenance costs.
